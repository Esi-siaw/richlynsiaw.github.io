Real-Time Threat Dashboard

This interactive geopolitical threat dashboard prototype was designed to provide timely visualization of security, governance, and misinformation risks across volatile regions.

Why It Matters

Amid increasing global volatility and information overload, decision-makers needed faster situational awareness tools to bridge the gap between detection and response.

Features

  • Real-time feeds with event tagging by risk severity
  • Heatmaps of geopolitical risks and active threats
  • Customizable stakeholder alerts and scenario projections

Impact

  • Used in program simulation exercises for crisis response planning
  • Supported early-warning briefings during political unrest
  • Formed the basis for external partner collaboration on OSINT data streams
