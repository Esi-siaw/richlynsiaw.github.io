Real-Time Protest Risk Dashboard

Integrated platform for early warning, crisis management, and reputational risk mitigation.

Project Overview

Developed and operationalized an in-house dashboard for monitoring protest activity and associated reputational risks. Designed for senior officials, security teams, and C-suite leadership, the tool combined real-time feeds with predictive analysis to guide interventions.

  • Live protest alerts with geospatial visualization
  • Integrated HUMINT, OSINT, and internal reporting
  • Cross-sector coordination with 20+ key stakeholders
  • Tailored messaging guidance for crisis response teams

This directly prevented reputational damage to both national stakeholders and private sector partners engaged in high-visibility sectors.
