Election Risk Framework Methodology

Strategic Challenge

NGOs and private sector actors required predictive risk assessment during volatile election cycles where traditional security advisories lacked contextual nuance.

Methodology

1. Indicator Matrix

15 weighted metrics tracking historical violence, candidate rhetoric, and disinformation trends.

2. Embedded Monitoring

Localized civil society nodes providing ground truth verification.

3. Decision Dashboard

Real-time visualization of risk heatmaps and recommended actions.

Key Results

  • 100% NGO safety compliance during high-risk periods
  • 12 market-entry risk briefs adopted by Fortune 500 firms
  • Early warning of 3 major protest events (72hr lead time)

Why This Matters

Bridged the gap between threat intelligence and operational decision-making during politically sensitive periods, enabling proactive security postures.
