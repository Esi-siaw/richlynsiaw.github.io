Election Risk Analysis Framework

Providing proactive risk assessments to support private sector continuity, humanitarian access, and governance resilience during electoral cycles.

📌 Context

During key electoral periods, especially in fragile or polarized environments, risks such as civil unrest, targeted misinformation, and infrastructure disruptions escalate. I designed tailored election risk assessments that provided **decision-makers, NGOs, and investors** with practical strategies to mitigate exposure.

⚙️ Methodology

  • 📊 **Risk Indicators:** Tracked 15+ variables including political stability, social media narratives, and violence triggers.
  • 🌍 **Regional Scanning:** Cross-referenced regional developments with local vulnerabilities for operational forecasting.
  • 🔍 **Private Sector Lens:** Mapped potential **supply chain disruptions**, reputational risks, and staff safety risks.
  • 🤝 **NGO Collaboration:** Integrated humanitarian operational priorities into analysis for access negotiation planning.

📈 Results & Impact

  • ✔️ Supported **contingency planning** for private sector clients managing sensitive supply chains during elections.
  • ✔️ Provided **early warning alerts** for NGOs operating in volatile electoral environments, improving safety and outreach success.
  • ✔️ Presented intelligence briefings to international partners focusing on the **impact of election violence on market entry risks**.

✅ Why It Matters

This framework ensured that **both global businesses** and **development partners** had actionable forecasts, bridging the gap between raw information and operational decisions.
