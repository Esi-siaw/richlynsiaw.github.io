🌍 Regional Threats Analysis Framework

This case study outlines a regional geopolitical analysis project I led, designed to help stakeholders navigate complex security, governance, and operational risks in volatile environments. While this particular project focused on West Africa, the methodology and analytical approach are fully adaptable to other regions including MENA, the Sahel, and other areas of strategic interest.

📌 Context:

During a period of heightened insecurity—including terrorism spillovers, political instability, and organised criminal networks—stakeholders required reliable, actionable regional threat assessments to guide not only national security policy, but also donor funding decisions, private sector planning, and humanitarian programming.

⚙️ My Approach:

  • Leveraged multi-source intelligence: open-source reporting, field observations, and confidential partner briefings
  • Mapped cross-border influence networks and identified key drivers of instability, including illicit trade corridors, extremist recruitment patterns, and coordinated disinformation campaigns
  • Produced forward-looking risk forecasts with scenario analysis adaptable to multiple regions and sectors

📊 Key Outputs:

  • Produced 3 quarterly regional threat assessments shared with government agencies, humanitarian partners, and private sector stakeholders
  • Developed influence maps highlighting high-risk border zones and transnational criminal flows affecting supply chains
  • Contributed to briefing materials presented during international diplomatic engagements and cross-sector consultations

✅ Results:

  • Enabled rerouting of operations in 4 vulnerable logistics corridors, reducing exposure for both humanitarian programs and private sector operations
  • Helped secure fund commitments in donor realignments based on evidence-based risk assessments
  • Improved coordination between governments, regional bodies, civil society, and international partners by providing common threat baselines for joint action

📌 Why This Matters:

Whether in West Africa, MENA, or elsewhere, regional geopolitical risks directly impact economic stability, operational planning, and public trust. My analysis frameworks are globally adaptable—designed not only to describe the environment but to actively shape decision-making across both public and private sector contexts.
