In strategic intelligence and policy advisory, understanding the influence and intent of key stakeholders is often more important than just tracking events. This is where actor mapping comes in.

My actor-mapping process is designed to identify key players, map their relationships, and anticipate their likely actions or reactions in a given geopolitical scenario.

  • Identify key actors influencing a decision or environment
  • Analyze relationships, alliances, and conflicts of interest between actors
  • Estimate probable courses of action or reactions from each actor based on interests and capacities

üß≠ Mapping Elements Include:

  • Relationships: Who do they collaborate with? Who opposes them?
  • Estimated Position: Supportive, neutral, or hostile to the subject matter at hand
